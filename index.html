Helping you Achieve your dream of becoming a great professional!
Wadamts Polytechnic is committed to providing high quality education leading to the award of National Diploma and Higher National Diploma Degree at very affordable fees. The Polytechnic is committed to producing broadly and thoroughly educated graduates who realize that their profession is not simply a trade to be learned but that it denotes a sense of social responsibility. Wadamts Polytechnic seeks applicants with demonstrated strong drive and desire to succeed and who are able to focus on the compassionate and the societal values of their profession. Our Polytechnic encourages its students to actively engage themselves in the community and to respond to the needs of the Society.

ACCREDITATION



Wadamts Polytechnic is a private polytechnic approved by the Ondo State Ministry of Education and accreditation process by the National Board for Technical Education(NBTE) in Nigeria has reached top gear . Aside from NYSC eligibility, Wadamts graduates will be eligible to practice in Nigeria and other parts of the world.
